About Us

Our Founders

Doug & Sherri Bean fell in love over a cup of coffee. Their first date was in this little coffee shop in their college town. They bothb were majoring in business and after they were married opened up The Coffee Cafe. Their siblings and family always came in to help out in the early days of the business, and still pop in today from time to time. They have two boys: Tom & Craig.

Favorite Parts of the Business

  • It is family oriented with actual family and employees that feel like family
  • Coming up with new drink ideas and being creative
  • Meeting new customers and hearing your stories
